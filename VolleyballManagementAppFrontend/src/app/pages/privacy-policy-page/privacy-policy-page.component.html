Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using the Volleyball Management App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your name, email, and phone number
  • Team and training participation data
  • Usage logs and preferences

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to manage teams, tournaments, trainings, and communication between users. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Protection

We apply industry-standard practices to keep your information secure, including encrypted storage and Auth0-based authentication.

4. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your data at any time by contacting the administrators or emailing us at volleyball@support.sch.bme.hu.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notices.
